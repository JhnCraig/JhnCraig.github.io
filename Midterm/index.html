Story Overview:

The story is set in Taishō-era Japan, where a secret organization called the Demon Slayer Corps has fought demons for centuries. Demons are former humans with supernatural abilities, including enhanced strength, rapid regeneration, and unique powers known as Blood Demon Arts. They can only be killed by sunlight, decapitation with Nichirin swords, or wisteria-based poison. The Demon Slayers, though human, use Breathing Styles, elemental techniques that grant superhuman abilities, to combat these demons. The most elite members are the Hashira, who have achieved exceptional skill and strength.
